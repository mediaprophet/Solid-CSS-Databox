# Solid Databox Credits

The Solid Databox is built upon the tireless, multi-generational efforts of advocates, engineers, researchers, and volunteers striving for a user-centric web. This document attempts to honor the deep lineage of this codebase.

## WebCivics & Solid Databox (2000–Present)
Led by the WebCivics community, the Databox extends early, decades-old foundational work in ontological mapping, verified civic engagement, and human-centric data architectures into the modern Solid ecosystem. 

## Community Solid Server (2019–2025)
This server was initially implemented as the "Community Solid Server" in TypeScript. We owe immense gratitude to the open-source contributors, as well as Inrupt Inc. and imec, who provided the early funding and engineering resources necessary to build the core modules.

## The Shoulders of Giants (1990s–Present)
This project is profoundly indebted to the foundational work of the **W3** and **W3C** communities. In particular, the architects of the **Read-Write-Web (RWW)**, **WebID**, and **Verifiable Credentials** movements.

We dedicate this codebase to the countless unpaid contributors who built the ideological and technical foundations of the decentralized web—including those pioneers who are sadly no longer with us. Your vision continues to guide us.
